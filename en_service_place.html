Art Museum

Cross-Sea Curating: The Song of Biei -Photography Exhibition

FindARTs assists a renowned international photographer in creating a private art museum in Hokkaido, Japan, titled “ The Song of Biei.” In the exhibition, photographer Chen Xiangyong presents his collection of photographs taken over the years in the Biei region of Hokkaido. Each photograph represents the photographer‘s unique perspective on nature, capturing every fleeting moment. The exhibition utilizes FindARTs’ lifelike tech to showcase more vibrant colors and delicate textures. In works such as “向詠と美瑛Toward Ei and Biei,” “冬日の美瑛Winter in Biei,” “殘雪の美瑛 Remnants of Snow in Biei,” and “日々の美瑛 Everyday Biei," we can observe the intricate details of the changing seasons in Hokkaido's Biei region.

Crescendo: Three Generations of Tshun-Guân Art Studio

The exhibition "Crescendo: Three Generations of Tshun-Guân Art Studio" showcases the creative splendor of the Tainan studio, spanning 115 years and three generations of artists. These artists include Pan Chun-yuan and his three descendants. However, due to its fragility, the original masterpiece "Ox Cart," one of Pan Chun-yuan's most significant paintings, cannot be exhibited. Therefore, FindARTs plays an essential role in the exhibition by presenting this magnificent artwork on our 75-inch art display.
