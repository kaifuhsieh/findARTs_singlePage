FindARTs x ART Taipei 2023 | "The Timeless Spectrum " Art Exhibition First Release "Digital Collection Masterpieces"

2023 / 10

FindARTs will participate in the ART Taipei 2023 Taipei International Art Fair from October 20th to October 23rd. With " The Timeless Spectrum " as the theme, it will showcase the works of 12 artists from classic to contemporary, presenting the spectrum of Taiwan's era across time and space. Through diverse mediums, textures, light, shadow, and creativity, the exhibition will convey stories and evoke emotions of different eras.

FindARTs is committed to serving the art community. The exhibition will showcase their long-standing services, including the distribution of digital collection masterpieces for original artworks, assisting artists and experienced collectors in customizing high-end art pieces. We also provide digital art curation solutions, allowing museums and curators to showcase original works remotely. Additionally, we offer art space solutions to help commercial spaces and corporate lobbies create an atmosphere of art and sophistication. FindARTs strives to provide the top-notch art service experience in various fields.

All FindARTs partners are cordially invited to attend and join in the celebration.
