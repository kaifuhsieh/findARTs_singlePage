蘇憲法《城市印象》 SU Hsien-Fa "City Impression"

蘇憲法《城市印象》

詩人畫筆藝術家 – 蘇憲法，身為東方的油畫家，期望藉由西方的媒彩與技法，融入東方的意境和精神，表達出中國傳統文化抽象的玄學概念，使創作產生在西方看不到、屬於東方境界的一種詩境。而從東方到西方，跑遍世界五大洲，觀察不同城市與建築樣貌，深刻感受城市記載發展的風貌。

SU Hsien-Fa "City Impression"

Poet and Painter - SU Hsien-Fa sought to infuse Eastern artistic conception into Western media and techniques in a manner that expresses the abstract philosophy of traditional Chinese culture, giving his artwork a poetry not found in the West. The poetry of urban colors and blocks emphasize the vastness of the cosmos.

城市掠影一巴黎

City Impression-Paris

藝術家從高處俯瞰整個巴黎市區，在水平且複雜綿密的建築群裡，凸顯出來的巴黎鐵塔，更讓人感受到這重要的視覺焦點，與做為城市代表的意象。天空的一朵烏雲形成對比，整個城市的風貌也一覽無遺。

The complex mosaic of downtown Paris serves as the backdrop to the Eiffel Tower, the symbol of the city. The cloud in the sky offers another point of contrast as well.

  • 蘇憲法
  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2016
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 80 x 116.5 cm

馬賽晨光序曲2

Song of Early Morning in Marseille2

  • 蘇憲法
  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2014
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 112 x 162 cm

法國南方普羅旺斯的馬賽港，藝術家描繪晨曦時的藍色光影，剛透出的陽光，反射出金黃色的古老建築；在暗黑的天空下，水面有著一道白光，與前方數百艘靜靜停著的遊艇，形成巧妙的畫面，感覺港口寧靜存在中透出的清新空氣。

Sunlight shining through the blue morning sky gives the old buildings a golden hue; the dark sky, light reflecting off the water and yachts at anchor hint at the serenity of the harbor.

  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2014

  • 53 x 72.5 cm

托斯卡尼山城

Mountain Town in Tuscany

  • 蘇憲法
  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2013
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 270 x 135 cm

藝術家用色塊鋪陳，展現山形跟古城的震撼感受；畫面以白色牆壁、紅色屋瓦的中古世紀建築群，應著前面的藍色樹林與後面的山形，再藉由橫跨從左到右的長弧構圖，讓山城綿延不斷的氣勢呈現在眼前。

The majesty of the mountains and historic city are captured through blocks of color. Medieval white walls and red tiled roofs run through the blue forest, and hills embody the enduring mountain town.

  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2013
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 80 x 116.5 cm

金波水鳥系列二

Wetland Series 2

  • 蘇憲法
  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2016
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 80 x 116.5 cm

在台灣中部海岸的高美濕地，黃昏日落映照在海平面的光澤、斑斑點點跟沙洲形成複雜的畫面，一隻水鳥孤獨的在沙洲上覓食，反射出藝術家於一個人獨處時、或者在靜下心來的一種心情。

The reflections of the setting sun, the complex patterns of the sandbar, and the lone bird foraging on the sands of Gaomei Wetland are reflection of solitude or inner peace.

  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2016

煙雨羅夢湖

Misty rain veiling the Loch Lomond

  • 蘇憲法
  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2010
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 116.5 x 80 cm

藝術家客居英國濕冷冬天時的創作，以東方墨色表現，描繪雨後冷冽寂靜的湖面，感受人文詩意的平靜。

The artist's creation during their stay in the cold and wet winter of England is depicted using oriental ink, portraying the chilly and quiet lake surface after the rain, evoking a sense of serene humanistic poetry.

馬賽晨光序曲

Song of Early Morning in Marseille

  • 蘇憲法
  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2014
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 112 x 162 cm

法國南方普羅旺斯的馬賽港，藝術家描繪清晨日出一開始的港口，即將普照的陽光，照著印著港口數百艘靜靜停著、等著甦醒啟航的遊艇，形成巧妙的畫面，感覺到一天即將展開的輕快序曲。

In Marseille's port, artists capture the sunrise as hundreds of yachts await their voyage under the soon-to-be radiant sunlight. The tranquil scene, skillfully crafted, sets the stage for a lively day to unfold.

  • SU Hsien-Fa
  • 2014
  • 油彩麻布 Oil on Canvas
  • 112 x 162 cm
